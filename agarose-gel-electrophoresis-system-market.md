<p><p>The agarose gel electrophoresis system market is divided into three main types: tray style, tape style, and dam style. Tray style systems feature a rigid platform to hold agarose gels, ensuring stable electrophoresis. Tape style systems utilize adhesive tape to secure the gel, making it easier to manipulate and transfer. Dam style systems involve barriers that contain the gel solution, enabling efficient separation and increased gel lifespan. Each type offers unique advantages tailored to specific laboratory needs and applications.</p></p>
