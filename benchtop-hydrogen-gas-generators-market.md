<p><ul><li>Traditional Alkaline Electrolyzer</li><li>PEM Electrolyzer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Benchtop hydrogen gas generators utilize two primary technologies: traditional alkaline electrolyzers and PEM (Proton Exchange Membrane) electrolyzers. Traditional alkaline electrolyzers use an alkaline solution to facilitate the electrolysis process, offering durability and cost-effectiveness, making them suitable for various applications. In contrast, PEM electrolyzers employ a solid polymer membrane, enabling higher efficiency and quicker response times, suitable for applications requiring high-purity hydrogen. Both technologies cater to diverse industries, including research, laboratory processes, and small-scale hydrogen production.</p></p>
