<p><strong>The Bone Conduction Hearing Implants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Behind the Ear (BTE)</li><li>In the Ear (ITE)</li><li>In the Canal (ITC)</li><li>Completely In Canal (CIC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bone conduction hearing implants are categorized based on their design and placement. Behind the Ear (BTE) devices are worn externally, connecting to the implant via a sound processor. In the Ear (ITE) models fit directly in the ear and offer a more discreet option. In the Canal (ITC) devices are smaller and partially fit within the ear canal, while Completely In Canal (CIC) models are the least visible, sitting entirely within the canal for a natural listening experience.</p></p>
