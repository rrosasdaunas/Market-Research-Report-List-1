<p><p>The bovine colostrum market can be categorized primarily into two types: spray dried and freeze dried colostrum powder. Spray dried colostrum powder involves rapidly drying colostrum using hot air, preserving its nutrients while creating a fine, stable product that is easy to mix with liquids. Freeze dried colostrum powder, on the other hand, involves freezing the colostrum and then removing moisture under vacuum, resulting in a product that retains more immune factors and enzymes, typically favored for its higher biological activity.</p></p>
