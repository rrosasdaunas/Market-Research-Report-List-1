<p><p>The centrifugal casting machine market is divided into two main types: vacuum and non-vacuum. Vacuum centrifugal casting machines operate by creating a vacuum environment, which minimizes oxidation and contamination, resulting in higher quality castings with improved mechanical properties. Non-vacuum machines, on the other hand, operate under atmospheric conditions, making them more straightforward and cost-effective but potentially yielding castings with lower quality and higher defects. Both types cater to various industries based on specific casting requirements and production processes.</p></p>
