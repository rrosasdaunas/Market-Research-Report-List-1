<p><strong>The Closed X-ray Tube Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Fixed anode X-ray Tube"</li><li>"Rotating anode X-ray Tube"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Closed X-ray Tube market comprises two primary types: fixed anode and rotating anode X-ray tubes. Fixed anode X-ray tubes feature a stationary target, making them simpler and less expensive, ideal for low-demand applications. In contrast, rotating anode X-ray tubes have a rotating target, allowing for higher heat dissipation and increased X-ray output, suitable for high-demand diagnostic imaging. Both types are essential for various medical and industrial applications, impacting image quality and efficiency in X-ray diagnostics.</p></p>
