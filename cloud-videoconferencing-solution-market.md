<p><p>The Cloud Videoconferencing Solution Market is characterized by two primary encoding types: Scalable Video Coding (SVC) and Advanced Video Coding (AVC). SVC allows for flexible video quality adaptation, enabling seamless adjustments in resolution based on varying network conditions, making it ideal for diverse user experiences. AVC, on the other hand, is widely used for its balance of compression efficiency and video quality, providing a stable experience for traditional videoconferencing needs. Both types cater to different user demands and network environments.</p></p>
