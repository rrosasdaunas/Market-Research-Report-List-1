<p><p>The commercial treadmill market is primarily divided into two types: manual treadmills and motorized treadmills. Manual treadmills operate without an electric motor, relying on the user's effort to move the belt, making them cost-effective and low-maintenance. They are suitable for various fitness environments. In contrast, motorized treadmills use an electric motor to adjust speed and incline, providing a more customizable workout experience. These treadmills are prevalent in gyms and fitness centers due to their advanced features and convenience.</p></p>
