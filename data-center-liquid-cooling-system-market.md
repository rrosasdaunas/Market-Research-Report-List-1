<p><strong>The Data Center Liquid Cooling System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Indirect Liquid Cooling System</li><li>Direct Liquid Cooling System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Data Center Liquid Cooling System market primarily consists of two types: Indirect Liquid Cooling Systems and Direct Liquid Cooling Systems. </p><p>Indirect Liquid Cooling Systems utilize a heat exchanger to transfer heat away from the equipment, using a secondary fluid to protect components from direct contact with the coolant. In contrast, Direct Liquid Cooling Systems involve the coolant making direct contact with the components, providing more efficient heat removal and allowing for higher density configurations. Both systems enhance cooling efficiency and reduce energy consumption in data centers.</p></p>
