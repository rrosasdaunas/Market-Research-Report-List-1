<p><p>The disposable medical latex gloves market is divided into two main types: powdered and non-powdered gloves. Powdered gloves are coated with cornstarch or a similar substance to facilitate easier donning, enhancing user comfort, especially in healthcare settings. However, they may pose risks of allergic reactions and contamination. Conversely, non-powdered gloves are free from such coatings, making them a safer choice for sensitive procedures and reducing the risk of allergens. Both types serve essential roles in infection control and hygiene practices.</p></p>
