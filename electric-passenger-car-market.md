<p><p>The electric passenger car market comprises two main types: Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEVs) and Battery Electric Vehicles (BEVs). PHEVs combine a traditional internal combustion engine with an electric motor, allowing for both electric-only driving and gasoline use, enhancing range and flexibility. BEVs, on the other hand, operate solely on electricity, relying on large battery packs for power, resulting in zero tailpipe emissions and requiring charging infrastructure. Both types cater to different consumer needs, promoting sustainability in transportation.</p></p>
