<p><p>Electric underfloor heating systems primarily consist of heating mats and heating cables. Heating mats are pre-assembled, electric heating elements laid out in a mesh, simplifying installation and providing uniform warmth across the floor surface. In contrast, heating cables are flexible, allowing for more customizable layouts, making them suitable for various room shapes and sizes. Both types deliver energy-efficient heating, enhancing comfort by radiating warmth evenly from the floor up, contributing to improved indoor temperature control and overall ambiance.</p></p>
