<p><strong>The Flexi-bag Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monolayer</li><li>Multilayer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flexi-bag market is primarily segmented into monolayer and multilayer types. Monolayer flexi-bags are made from a single type of polymer, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness, ideal for products with less stringent barrier requirements. In contrast, multilayer flexi-bags consist of multiple layers of different materials, providing enhanced barrier properties, durability, and protection against moisture, gases, and light. This makes multilayer bags suitable for sensitive products like chemicals and food items, ensuring longer shelf life and quality preservation.</p></p>
