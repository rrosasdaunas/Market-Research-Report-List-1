<p><p>The food tray sealer market is categorized into three main types: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manual sealers require operator intervention to seal trays, making them suitable for small operations. Semi-automatic sealers streamline the process, allowing operators to load trays while the machine performs sealing, enhancing efficiency for medium-scale businesses. Fully automatic sealers provide high-speed, hands-free operation, ideal for large production environments, optimizing workflow and reducing labor costs while ensuring consistent sealing quality.</p></p>
