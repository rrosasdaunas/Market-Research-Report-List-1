<p><strong>The Forklift Scale Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wired Forklift Scale</li><li>Wireless Forklift Scale</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The forklift scale market comprises two main types: wired and wireless forklift scales. Wired forklift scales connect directly to the forklift's electrical system, providing reliable data transmission but may limit mobility due to cables. In contrast, wireless forklift scales use radio frequency or Bluetooth technology to transmit weight data to a display unit, offering greater flexibility and ease of use without the clutter of wires. Both types are essential for accurate weighing in logistics and warehouse operations.</p></p>
