<p><p>The handset display market is primarily categorized into several types: </p><p>1. **LCD (Liquid Crystal Display)** - Offers good color reproduction and brightness but less contrast. </p><p>2. **LED (Light Emitting Diode)** - Uses LED backlighting for LCDs, enhancing brightness and energy efficiency. </p><p>3. **IPS (In-Plane Switching)** - A type of LCD providing wider viewing angles and better color accuracy. </p><p>4. **OLED (Organic Light Emitting Diode)** - Delivers superior contrast and deeper blacks by emitting light at the pixel level. </p><p>5. **Others** - Encompasses emerging technologies and variations not fitting the above categories.</p></p>
