<p><strong>The Immersion Cooling Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Phase Immersion Cooling</li><li>Two-Phase Immersion Cooling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The immersion cooling market is divided into two main types: Single-Phase and Two-Phase Immersion Cooling. Single-Phase Immersion Cooling involves submerging electronic components in a coolant that remains in a liquid state, effectively dissipating heat. In contrast, Two-Phase Immersion Cooling utilizes a coolant that transitions between liquid and vapor phases, allowing for efficient heat transfer and cooling through evaporation and condensation processes. Both types are increasingly adopted in data centers and high-performance computing environments for their superior thermal management and energy efficiency.</p></p>
