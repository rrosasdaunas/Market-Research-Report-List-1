<p><p>The laptop charger market can be broadly categorized into two main types: AC (Alternating Current) chargers and DC (Direct Current) chargers. AC chargers convert wall socket electricity into the voltage and current required by laptops, making them the primary charging option for home and office use. On the other hand, DC chargers are often used in automotive or portable power solutions, allowing laptops to recharge in vehicles or through battery packs. Each type serves distinct usage scenarios, catering to varying consumer needs.</p></p>
