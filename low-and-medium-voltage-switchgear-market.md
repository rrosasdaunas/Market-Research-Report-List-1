<p><strong>The Low and Medium Voltage Switchgear Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>GIS</li><li>AIS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Low and Medium Voltage switchgear market comprises two primary types: Gas-Insulated Switchgear (GIS) and Air-Insulated Switchgear (AIS). GIS utilizes gas-filled compartments for better insulation and reduced footprint, making it ideal for urban settings. AIS, on the other hand, relies on air for insulation and is more commonly used in outdoor applications due to its simplicity and lower cost. Both types serve essential roles in managing electrical power distribution and ensuring system reliability across various industries.</p></p>
