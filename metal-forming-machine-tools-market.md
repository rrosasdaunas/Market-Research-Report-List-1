<p><p>The metal forming machine tools market is categorized into three primary types: bulk forming, sheet forming, and powder metal forming. </p><p>Bulk forming involves shaping metal into desired forms through processes like forging and extrusion, ideal for producing large components. Sheet forming focuses on manipulating flat metal sheets using techniques such as stamping and bending, suitable for automotive and appliance manufacturing. Powder metal forming utilizes powdered materials compacted and sintered to create intricate shapes, often used in producing complex parts with high precision in various industries.</p></p>
