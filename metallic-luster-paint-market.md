<p><strong>The Metallic Luster Paint Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent Based</li><li>Water Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metallic luster paint market is primarily segmented into solvent-based and water-based types. Solvent-based metallic luster paints offer durability and a high sheen finish, making them suitable for exterior applications and industrial use. They tend to have a stronger odor and longer drying times. Conversely, water-based metallic luster paints are more environmentally friendly, featuring lower volatile organic compounds (VOCs). They provide easier cleanup and quicker drying, making them ideal for interior applications and DIY projects, appealing to eco-conscious consumers.</p></p>
