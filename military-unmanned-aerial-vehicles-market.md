<p><p>The Military Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAV) market is divided into two primary types: Fixed Wing and Rotary Wing. Fixed Wing UAVs, resembling traditional aircraft, are designed for long-range missions and offer higher endurance and speed, making them suitable for surveillance and reconnaissance. In contrast, Rotary Wing UAVs, or drones with rotating blades, excel in vertical takeoff and landing, offering agility and the ability to operate in confined spaces, often used for tactical operations and real-time intelligence gathering.</p></p>
