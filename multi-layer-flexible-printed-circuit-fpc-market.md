<p><p>The Multi-layer Flexible Printed Circuit (FPC) market encompasses two primary types based on adhesive use. Circuits with adhesive utilize bonding agents to layer multiple flexible substrates, enhancing mechanical stability and durability for complex applications. In contrast, circuits without adhesive employ methods like laser welding or conductive inks to achieve layers, resulting in lighter designs and improved electrical performance. Both types serve diverse industries, including consumer electronics, automotive, and medical devices, each chosen for specific functionalities and manufacturing requirements.</p></p>
