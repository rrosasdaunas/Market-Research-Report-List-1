<p><strong>The Optical Liquid Point Level Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact</li><li>Contactless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical liquid point level sensor market is segmented into contact and contactless types. Contact sensors operate by directly interacting with the liquid, detecting level changes using light transmission through the medium. They are typically reliable for clear liquids. In contrast, contactless sensors utilize light beams to detect levels without any direct contact with the liquid. This method is advantageous for corrosive or hazardous substances, as it minimizes the risk of contamination and damage, offering a wider range of applications.</p></p>
