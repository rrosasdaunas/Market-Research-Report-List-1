<p><p>Package on Package (PoP) is an advanced packaging technology used to vertically stack multiple integrated circuits (ICs) for improved performance and space efficiency. The market comprises Traditional PoP, which includes separate packages stacked together; PSfcCSP, which combines package-on-package and chip-scale packaging; Through-Mold-Via (TMV), allowing electrical connections through the mold to save space; and Exposed-die TMV, which exposes the die for thermal management and compact design. Each type caters to specific applications in consumer electronics and networking.</p></p>
