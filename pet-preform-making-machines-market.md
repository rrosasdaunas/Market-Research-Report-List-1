<p><p>PET preform making machines are categorized into four main types based on their operational mechanisms: hydraulic, mechanical, electric, and pneumatic. Hydraulic machines employ fluid power for molding, offering high force and flexibility. Mechanical machines rely on conventional mechanical systems, providing durability and cost-effectiveness. Electric machines utilize electric motors for precise control and energy efficiency, appealing for high-production environments. Pneumatic machines operate on compressed air, allowing for lighter designs and quicker cycle times, ideal for specific production needs.</p></p>
