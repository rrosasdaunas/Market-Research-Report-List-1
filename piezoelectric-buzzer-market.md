<p><p>The piezoelectric buzzer market is categorized into active and passive types. Active piezo buzzers generate sound internally when an electrical signal is applied, making them simpler to use since they don't require an external oscillator. In contrast, passive piezo buzzers rely on an external signal for operation, producing sound based on the frequency of that signal. Both types serve various applications in electronics, ranging from alarms and notifications to indicators, with distinct operational characteristics suited to specific uses.</p></p>
