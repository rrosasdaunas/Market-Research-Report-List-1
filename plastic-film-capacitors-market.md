<p><strong>The Plastic Film Capacitors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axial Plastic Film Capacitors</li><li>Radial Plastic Film Capacitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Plastic Film Capacitors market is primarily segmented into Axial and Radial types. Axial Plastic Film Capacitors feature leads extending from each end, allowing for easier placement in tight spaces, making them suitable for through-hole applications. Radial Plastic Film Capacitors, on the other hand, have leads protruding from one side, facilitating a more compact layout on printed circuit boards. Both types are valued for their stability, low dielectric losses, and ability to handle high voltages, catering to diverse electronic applications.</p></p>
