<p><p>The biliary stents market is divided into two main types: plastic and metal biliary stents. Plastic biliary stents are typically used for temporary relief of bile duct obstructions, offering a cost-effective and easily deployable solution, although they may require replacement due to potential clogging. In contrast, metal biliary stents, generally made of stainless steel or nitinol, are designed for longer-term use, providing greater durability and reduced risk of occlusion, making them suitable for chronic conditions.</p></p>
