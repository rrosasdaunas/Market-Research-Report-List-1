<p><ul><li>Air-Supported Structures</li><li>Air-Inflated Structures</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pneumatic structures market encompasses various types of constructions utilizing air pressure for stability and shape maintenance. Air-supported structures rely on a constant internal airflow to maintain their shape, often used in sports arenas and temporary shelters. In contrast, air-inflated structures utilize air to create a firm, self-supporting framework, typically consisting of inflated membranes that provide insulation and weather protection, making them suitable for a range of applications like warehouses and exhibition spaces. Both types offer lightweight and adaptable solutions.</p></p>
