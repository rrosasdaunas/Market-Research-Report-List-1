<p><ul><li>Air Jet Nebulizers</li><li>Ultrasonic Nebulizer</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable compressor nebulizer market primarily comprises three types: air jet nebulizers, ultrasonic nebulizers, and others. Air jet nebulizers use compressed air to convert liquid medication into a mist, making it easy to inhale. Ultrasonic nebulizers utilize high-frequency vibrations to produce aerosolized medication, offering quieter operation and finer mist. Other types may include mesh nebulizers, which employ a vibrating mesh to disperse the medication. Each type serves different user needs, influencing preferences based on efficiency, noise level, and portability.</p></p>
