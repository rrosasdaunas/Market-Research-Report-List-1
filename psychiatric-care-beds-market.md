<p><p>The psychiatric care beds market comprises three main types: electric beds, semi-electric beds, and manual beds. Electric beds offer full electronic control for patient positioning, enhancing comfort and safety. Semi-electric beds provide motorized adjustments for the back and legs while allowing manual height adjustments. Manual beds rely entirely on hand-operated mechanisms, offering a cost-effective solution but requiring more effort for repositioning. Each type caters to different patient needs and facility budgets, impacting their adoption in mental health care settings.</p></p>
