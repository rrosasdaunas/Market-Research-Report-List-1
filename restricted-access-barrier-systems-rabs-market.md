<p><p>Restricted Access Barrier Systems (RABS) are vital in ensuring the protection of aseptic operations in pharmaceutical manufacturing. They allow for controlled access to critical areas, minimizing contamination risk. The market for RABS is divided into two primary types: Passive RABS, which rely on physical barriers and airflow to maintain sterility, and Active RABS, which incorporate active components like ventilated hoods and integrated monitoring systems for enhanced safety. Both types are essential for meeting regulatory standards and ensuring product integrity.</p></p>
