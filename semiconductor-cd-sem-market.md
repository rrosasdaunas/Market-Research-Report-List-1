<p><strong>The Semiconductor CD-SEM Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Photomask CD-SEM</li><li>Wafer CD-SEM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Semiconductor CD-SEM market is divided into two main types: Photomask CD-SEM and Wafer CD-SEM. Photomask CD-SEM focuses on measuring critical dimensions on photomasks, which are templates used in lithography for semiconductor manufacturing. This ensures accuracy before the actual wafer production. In contrast, Wafer CD-SEM measures critical dimensions directly on the wafer during the production process, helping to maintain quality control and optimize fabrication. Both types are essential for ensuring precision in semiconductor device manufacturing.</p></p>
