<p><strong>The Solvent Reuse Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>On-site Solvent Recycling</li><li>Off-site Solvent Recycling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solvent reuse market is divided into on-site and off-site solvent recycling. On-site solvent recycling involves facilities processing solvents within their premises, ensuring immediate reuse, reducing transport costs, and minimizing downtime. Off-site solvent recycling, on the other hand, occurs at dedicated recycling facilities where solvents are collected, processed, and returned to users. While on-site recycling offers convenience and control, off-site recycling benefits from specialized processes and economies of scale, often making it suitable for larger operations or diverse solvent types.</p></p>
