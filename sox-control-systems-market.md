<p><p>The SOx Control Systems market focuses on technologies designed to reduce sulfur oxide emissions from industrial processes. Two primary methods are Selective Non-Catalytic Reduction (SNCR) and Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR). SNCR involves the injection of reagents at high temperatures to convert SOx into nitrogen and water, while SCR employs catalysts to enhance the reaction efficiency at lower temperatures. Both methods serve various industries, including power generation and manufacturing, to meet environmental regulations and improve air quality.</p></p>
