<p><p>Tumor ablation devices market comprises various techniques, including microwave ablation, laser ablation, and ultrasound ablation. Microwave ablation utilizes electromagnetic waves to generate heat, effectively destroying tumor cells. Laser ablation employs focused light to target and vaporize tumors with precision. Ultrasound ablation utilizes high-frequency sound waves to induce localized heating, leading to tissue destruction. Each method offers unique advantages, such as minimally invasive procedures, precise targeting, and reduced recovery times, making them increasingly popular in oncology treatment options.</p></p>
