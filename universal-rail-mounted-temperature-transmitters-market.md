<p><strong>The Universal Rail Mounted Temperature Transmitters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>2-Wire</li><li>3-Wire</li><li>4-Wire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Universal rail-mounted temperature transmitters are categorized based on their wiring configurations: 2-wire, 3-wire, and 4-wire. </p><p>- **2-Wire** transmitters use a single pair of wires for both power and signal transmission, making them cost-effective and simple to install, but may experience some voltage drop. </p><p>- **3-Wire** designs are more accurate by compensating for resistance in the wire, improving measurement precision. </p><p>- **4-Wire** transmitters offer the highest accuracy, using two wires for power and two for signal, ideal for long-distance applications where precision is critical.</p></p>
