<p><p>The veterinary splints market comprises two primary types: inflatable and non-inflatable splints. They are lightweight and easy to apply, making them suitable for various conditions. Non-inflatable splints, on the other hand, are typically made from rigid materials designed to provide stable support to fractures or injuries, ensuring minimal movement. Both types play crucial roles in animal rehabilitation and injury management.</p></p>
