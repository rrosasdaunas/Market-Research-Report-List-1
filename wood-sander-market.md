<p><p>The wood sander market is categorized mainly into orbital sanders, sheet sanders, and others. Orbital sanders, known for their circular sanding pads, offer a swirl-free finish and are ideal for various surfaces. Sheet sanders, or finish sanders, use rectangular sheets and excel in detailed work and tight spaces. Other types include belt sanders, disk sanders, and palm sanders, each serving specific applications. Together, these tools cater to diverse woodworking needs, enhancing efficiency and achieving desired surface finishes.</p></p>
